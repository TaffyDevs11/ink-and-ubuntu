About Us Ink & Ubuntu

To empower individuals and organizations to express their stories through impactful, purpose-driven design.

At Ink & Ubuntu, we believe every brand has a story worth telling — authentically and powerfully. Rooted in the African philosophy of Ubuntu — “I am because we are” — we craft intentional, human-centered designs that connect people to purpose.

Why Choose Us?

  • We co-create through partnership and listening.
  • We celebrate culture and connection in every detail.

Whether you're building a brand, launching a project, or refreshing your identity, we’re here to walk the journey with you — blending creativity, culture, and collaboration to bring your vision to life.

1. What makes Ink & Ubuntu different from other design studios?

At Ink & Ubuntu, we blend creativity with cultural storytelling. Our designs are narrative-driven, deeply collaborative, and crafted with intention — not just to look good, but to say something. We also offer multilingual service (English, French, and Shona), allowing us to connect meaningfully with diverse clients around the world.

2. What kind of clients do you work with?

We work with entrepreneurs, creatives, professionals, and small-to-mid-sized businesses looking for personalized design solutions. Whether you're launching a brand, planning a celebration, or updating your visual identity, we tailor our services to meet your unique goals.

3. How long does a typical design project take?

Timelines vary depending on the complexity of the project. For example, a custom logo might take 7–10 business days, while a full brand package or visual report could take 2–4 weeks. We’ll always discuss timelines with you up front during the planning phase.

4. Do you offer revisions or design edits?

Absolutely! All of our packages include a set number of revisions to make sure you're happy with the final product. Our process is collaborative — your feedback is key to getting the design just right.

5. How do I get started or request a quote?

You can fill out our online Client Questionnaire or reach out via our Contact page. Once we understand your needs, we’ll provide a custom quote and walk you through the next steps to begin your project.
